Donor Eligibility

  1. Donor should be of the age from 17 years to 60 years.
  2. Donor should be of more than 45kg.
  3. Donor should have 12gm or more hameoglobin in the blood.
  4. Donor should be in good health and should not suffered from any other serious illness.
  5. Donor must be previously confirmed positive for COVID-19 by a laboratory test.
  6. Donor must be fully recovered from the virus and symptoms free for atleast 28 days.

Please note: a history of donating blood isnot required.
