What is convalescent plasma?


Convalescent plasma is collected from someone who has recovered from a virus. When a person is infected with a virus, their body starts making antibodies to fight it. It is belived these antibodies could be the key ingredient for a treatment to help others with a same virus.

Who can donate?

To participate as a convalescent plasma donor in the trails, you must meet the current plasma donor eligibility criteria and you must be:

  1. Previously confirmed positive for COVID-19 by a laboratory test.
  2. Younger than 67 years old and
  3. Fully recovered from the virus and symptoms free for atleast 28 days.

Please Note: History of blood donation isnot required.
